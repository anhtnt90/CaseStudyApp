What Does it Take to Create a Doctor Appointment App

Kyrylo Savytskyi | 18 Mar 2019

Everyone knows how difficult it is to make an appointment with a doctor. Developing "find a doctor app" for medical centers, beauty clinics, and dentistry is a good way to improve the quality of services, stand out among competitors and gain the trust of customers. Doctors app is must-have for public and private medicine, as well as the pharmaceutical business! Since such applications contribute to the effective interaction between the doctor and the patient.

But how to create a find a doctor appointment app? Let's find out!

The statistics say that at least 35% of smartphone owners use mobile applications to help monitor their health. This can be just one application, for example, medicine search, or several medical programs for Android or iOS at once.

How effective are they? It depends on the application type, how user-friendly the user interface is, and how seriously the user takes it. Experts predict that by next year the number of healthcare applications users will exceed 500 million.

Main features of healthcare apps

There is a high level of competition in the medical market because patients carefully choose which clinic to entrust their health. The professionalism of the doctors and the service of the clinic is the decisive factor in the selection.

Nice developed medical doctor app will help clients of the clinic to interact with it easily and save their time. It is also important that the application reliably protect patient data and medical confidentiality.

A mobile application is a virtual medical record with various capabilities that will make it easy and convenient to take care of your health. But what features should it include?

Book a doctor appointment

The patient can enroll at any time from any place without having to call the clinic. A booking can be moved or canceled;

Chat with a professional

Sometimes just a couple of messages is enough. If user has just a couple of questions, or would like to learn more about his state - he can just send standard or or video message to a specialist inside the application, without an offline appointment

Clinic or doctors search

Medical application should allow users to browse clinics around them, view other user reviews, check the schedule and list of specialists, and more

Specialists

Information about doctors, schedule and place of admission, rating, and reviews of patients, recommendations

Call a doctor

If it is not possible to come to the clinic, the patient can call the doctor through the doctor scheduling app. A specialist can describe his symptoms, as well as appoint a specific time of the visit;

Medical history

The patient can view past and future doctor's appointments, treatment prescription, medical examinations and tests directly in the application;

Family account

The ability to add a child or a close relative to your account will allow customers to take care of their loved ones;

Reviews and rating system

After visiting the doctor, the patient can evaluate his work;

Payment for services through the application

The patient can pay for the clinic services from his personal account in the application or by credit card.

Additional features:

  • Medication data, its reviews prices, and more.
  • Database. Obtaining detailed information about the medicine, its value by pointing the camera of the smartphone at the packaging barcode.
  • Patient's health log that helps to monitor vital parameters (blood pressure, pulse, blood glucose data).
  • Push Notifications about changes, news, promotions, events, new arrivals, etc.

Business and monetization models doctor booking app

How can online doctor app be useful for business?

Increase in profits.

The application for doctor appointment is a new tool for increasing sales and returning visitors. The easier it is for the patient to interact with the booking system of the clinic, the more readily he will come back.

Customer loyalty

Offer your patients a convenient and affordable service to take care of their health, provide individual discounts. Your own application will help you to learn more about users, find out what they need and what they are looking for. Application will allow you to contact your users as well: reminding about upcoming or missing appointments, asking users if they need additional help, or how recovery is going, and much more.

Automating staff

Simple arithmetic: the more entries through the application, the fewer calls to the registry. Of course, there will be conservative patients for whom call is easier, however, administrators will be able to manage easily.

Personal offers

The data from the mobile application will help you to get to know your customers better and offer promotions interesting to them. For example, offer your patients who did not use your services for a long time to be examined by a doctor at a reduced price. It will definetely help you to attract at least half of the non-active patients.

Feedback

You can learn about the quality of the work of doctors and clinic staff by collecting feedback through the application. You can quickly correct problems, and patients will be pleased to know that their opinion is important to you.

Competitive advantage

Own mobile application will highlight you among other medical centers and will be another argument in the choice of your clinic.

Usually, doctor appointment app that is made for one particular clinic is available for free. It helps to generate new patients flow, which helps increase profits.

Applications that work as a catalog of different clinics usually use ads to make money. Besides, they can have individual contracts with various clinics. It allows earning money by helping each of them to increase the number of new patients, return existing users, and more.

Another way of monetization is offering in-app payments for rendered services, or in-app store that would help people to buy medications, pay for appointments, and so on.

Some mobile doctor applications generate profits using a paid subscription. For example, each doctor could pay a monthly membership fee. Depending on your business model, you can allow only doctors with paid subscriptions in the app. Or to show premium doctors higher in searches, and help them to find more clients with in-app ads. In this case, doctors will be happy to pay the fee, because it will allow them to increase their client base, and to market their services easier then in offline.

Mobile doctor app estimations

Estimating the costs and timeframes for each particular stage is a difficult task. To do this, we need to be well aware of the final result and be technically savvy in order to estimate the labor costs for each function. Therefore, most experienced specialists are usually involved in this process.
Usually, the app development cost estimate occurs in two stages:

1. Preliminary cost estimate

Although the preliminary estimations are called “rough,” it allows one to know with sufficient accuracy the necessary amount of investments needed to create an application before signing any contracts. That is, you can get it within one day max.

2. The exact price for application development

A more accurate assessment of the cost of developing applications includes estimations for each particular feature: Welcome Screen, Login Screen, Registration, and so on. We calculate how much time is needed for user interface design and development, business logic development, back-end API server, and so on. It helps us to make an extremely precise estimation, that will be used to make a final offer. It is supported by a contract, which fixates the dates and stages of work with a detailed description of the final result.

How much time does it take to develop an app?

The process of creating a doctor phone app for iOS, Android and other platforms consists of several successive stages. Terms at each stage may be different depending on the complexity of work specifically for each case.

  • Free Estimate (~1 day)
  • Detailed study of the problem, analysis of the market and competitors (1-3 days)
  • Prototyping Writing technical specifications (5-15 days)
  • Design (5-15 days)
  • Programming (depends on the project, number of features, complexity, and so on)
  • Testing (just like Programming, this stage depends a lot on project complexity, and is unique to each project)
  • Deployment

On average, doctor appointment application development for one platform (iOS or Android) can take around four months:

  • user interface development: at least ~200 hours
  • business logic development: at least ~350 hours
  • back-end development: at least 250 hours

If you decide to make an app for both platforms, it will add an additional 500 hours, depending on the project complexity. With the second platform, we will be able to save much time on reusable parts of the project, such as back-end and admin panel.

Development process

The development of a mobile application from scratch to release takes place on average in 7 basic steps. Some of them are pretty simple and can be done in a couple of days. Others might 3 months, or even more. Some steps can be performed in parallel and some only sequentially.

Below are the stages of the full cycle of mobile apps development from the planning to the release to stores.

Planning

Each project participant must clearly understand the task. To do this, we make a mind map, which clearly demonstrates all the product functions and prototypes, which reflect all the application screens and the transition scheme for them. Depending on our needs, prototypes can be static or interactive.

Design

After the approval of the prototypes, we take up the design of the application. After all, it is essential that the application is beautiful, functional and contains unique features that distinguish your application from competitors. We carefully draw each screen, taking into account the characteristics of the platform.

Mobile App Development

Directly developing a mobile application consists of three stages. At each stage, the customer is involved in the process in order to judge the status of the project independently and try how it works.

You must select a platform for development. Android mobile development or iOS mobile development, or both.

Server Development (backend)

The back-end is the core of the project. It helps us to save and retrieve the data from the database, authorize users. The back-end is where the core logic of the project lives in. It is essential to write the API and all the accompanying documentation and keep it in good condition.

Testing

Automatic and manual testing on different devices. It is important to pay great attention to the quality of each feature present in the application and to test them under different conditions. Users never use the app in an ideal environment, so we need to see how the app works without internet or on a slow connection. What happens if the user turns off the phone right in the middle of important calculations? Moreover, how the app will work in a “Low Power” mode, or when the battery is almost drained.

Publication to App Store and Play Market

Promotion and Marketing

Marketing is extremely important and often forgotten part of the process. Without users, the project won’t live and obviously won’t generate any revenue. Marketing can be started even before the development itself. You can contact the media, create a website with additional info about the project, begin gathering emails of early adopters, attract people in social media, and listen to their feedback.

Popular doctor appointment applications

Practo

This application will be useful for both a user and his relatives and friends. You may not have suspected that going to the doctors may be completely relaxed and effective. Moreover, through Practo, one can sign up for consultations with individual doctors, general examinations in clinics, spas, etc. You can get the best advice from doctors and experts in your city and share information with others.

Thanks to the search capabilities, users can search for doctors depending on the area, the availability of the institution, and even symptoms. By the way, you can consult online, which is very important for those who can not get to the hospital for several reasons. More than 30,000 qualified doctors of 20 specialties are gathered here. As well as a feature of the doctor appointment online app is that you have access to useful selected articles from experts in the field of health.

ZocDoc

ZocDoc is a medical technology company that allows patients to find and make an appointment with a doctor through a mobile application or online. The company's management claims that the average waiting time for a doctor is 19 days; the goal of Zocdoc is that patients can meet with the doctor after 24 hours.

A big problem in this area is inefficient planning methods and a high percentage of canceling or rescheduling a doctor's appointment. Having solved these issues, ZocDoc managed to achieve its goal. The company earns a subscription fee of $ 3000/year, which is paid by doctors and provides free access to patients.

Thanks to the law “On Protection of Patients and Affordable Care”, by 2025 there may be a shortage of more than 100,000 doctors, which will make the ZocDoc service even more in demand.

Each month, the company attracts more than 2.5 million unique visits.
The ZocDoc platform is conveniently built for both doctors and clients with a variety of filters to find the best option. A special approach to customers and a focus on fulfilling all their needs leaves no chance for customers not to return for ZocDoc services
